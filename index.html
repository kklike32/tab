What Is the T.A.B?


Have you ever felt uncomfortable temperatures while sleeping? Has this made you take extraordinary measures to control the temperature of your room, such as a fan or opening a window? Well, it's not a problem anymore. With the newest product in the sleep world, you’ll be able to sleep soundly. T.A.B., or Temperature Adjusting Blanket, is the product for you. This temperature controlled blanket is connected to an app the user can download on his or her phone. From the app the user can choose a certain temperature for the blanket. The app will send a signal to the blankets control box and that control box will heat the pad inside the blanket. Once the user goes to sleep, the blanket will be the perfect temperature for them. Additionally, if the user is uncomfortable with the heat, they can change it right then and there in bed. The internal components of the blanket are fairly simple. There are a total of three layers, the top layer blanket, the heating cord, and the bottom layer blanket. The heating cord will have thermal and cooling beads attached to the cord, which is attached to the control box. After receiving a signal from the app the control box will start heating up the cord inside the blanket which will heat or cool down the beads. The heating and cooling process will be done by a motor in the control box. For optimal comfort, we decided to attach the small control box to the blanket so the user won’t have to move around a bunch of parts.
